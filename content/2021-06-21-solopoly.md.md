Work done under the direction of Michael Kempson, one of Australia's premier printmakers. Based on the number 16 for a student book, this so-called "world's best tattoo" is an intaglio etching created with multiple techniques and some paintstaking minute details. The remaining edition in my hands is in the aforementioned book and some prints were distributed.
