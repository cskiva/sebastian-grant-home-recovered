Created by a mixed media approach, starting from the left hand side watercolour, this work is a tableau of various encouraging things. Keenly pointing towards the future, this was a colour expression of the fugue state ink drawing dominating this period. Sold to my patron and friend and Ty, this work contains imagery of the wolf and woman to create a feeling of what we need to protect and how to protect it.
