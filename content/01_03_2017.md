###### Boneless Cafe Bermagui - Mantra/Poster.

Commission from vegan cafe owners to bring attention to their unique brand of morning ritual. Stained with a tea-bag and with burnt corners - a technique learnt from old British television show; *Art* *Attack*.

\
Original displayed on the street in a notice board holder.
