A dyptych painted for the end of art school in 2011. The Oil Painting was projected from a freestyled drawing and embellished on the fly. Representing the asethetic of digital colonialism before that was actually a thing (but very much a creative domain). It sits on an old-school surreal foundation of New south wales coastlines populated by symbols in an OS-like layout.\
\
This image is a snippet of the full work, which was half destroyed and half given to The Outlook.
